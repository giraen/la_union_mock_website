DESTINATIONS

  • SAN JUAN

    Considered to be the Surfing Capital of the Northern Philippines. Popular for its surfing and other water sports activities.

  • TANGADAN FALLS

    One of the most popular eco-based tourist spots in San Juan, La Union. Provide a perfect respite for both locals and travelers during summer.

  • MA CHO TEMPLE

    Built in 1975 in dedication to the Chinese goddess Matzu, known as the protector of the sea. That's why the temple sits facing the sea.

  • GRAPE FARMS

    If you go to Baung, La Union, you'll have all the time to pick fresh grapes. There are several farms in the area.

  • BULALAKAW FALLS

    Bulalakaw falls is one of the highest falls in La Union, measuring around 30-50 meters high. It has a serene and veneer pool with icy-cold and crystal-clear waters.

  • PEBBLE BEACH

    Pebbles of different sizes cover a long stretch of the beach.It is a beautiful sight to see especially if you come here during the early morning and late afternoon.
